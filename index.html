Number Guessing Game

    Instruction

  1. The player will input a maximum number, which sets the range of numbers from 1 to the chosen maximum (minimum: 25, maximum: 75).
  2. The computer will generate a random number within the range of 1 to the player’s chosen maximum number.
  3. The player will have up to 5 attempts to guess the random number generated by the computer.
  4. After each guess, the computer will indicate whether the guessed number is greater or lessen than the random number.
  5. If the player guesses the number correctly, they earn 1 point.
  6. If the player fails to guess the number within 5 attempts, the computer earns 1 point.


    7. Enjoy the game and don’t feel pressured. Just like in life, it’s okay to make mistakes because every wrong guess brings you closer to success. Have fun and keep trying!
